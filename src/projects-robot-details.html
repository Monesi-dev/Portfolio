Smart Tank

This tiny and cute robot is actually a smart tank, equipped with a wide variety of sensors that enable this little bungle of wires and cables to perform a great number of incredible feats.

This strong tank has a distance sensor at the front that being mounted on a servomotor is free to rotate 90 degress to his right and also his left. This is a safe and reliable way to detect possible obstacles on its trajectory, and therefore, avoid them.

Moreover, on his shoulder are mounted two light sensors that are able to guide the robot to the nearest source of light in an instant.
